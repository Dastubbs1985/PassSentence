Instructions for installation.

Step 1: Download the .zip file.
Step 2: Unzip the file to a folder on the Desktop.
Step 3: Load the 'Google Chrome' Browser.
Step 4: Click on the 3 dots in the top right corner of the browser (Just under the 'Close' button in the top right corner).
Step 5: Navigate to the 'Extensions' page.
Step 6: Enable 'Developer Mode'.
Step 7: Click on the 'Load unpacked extension...' button and navigate to the folder on the desktop created in Step 2.


PassSentence in now ready for use.
